Know More About Allen Ren

Early Experiences & Social Activities & Award Records

Early Experiences

Allen was born in Qingdao, Shandong Province on April 11, 1989

  • Ex-Table Tennis Player
  • At the age of 6, he began to practice table tennis and trained hard every day.
    At the age of 13, he entered the Shandong Provincial team with excellent results.
    At the age of 16, he retired due to injury.
  • Former Airport Staff
  • Allen then focused on study and worked in Qingdao Liuting Airport after graduation.
    He is always hard-working, serious and willing to work,.
    The airport also issued the "learning Allen Ren's work spirit" advocacy.
  • New Path To Life
  • Because of his love of music and dance, Allen uses his spare time to learn singing and dancing,
    and participates in various talent competitions to exercise himself.
    Won the support of his family, officially resigned from the airport, and started another life.

Social Activities

  1. On September 9, 2017, Allen attended the 2017 Bazaar Star Charity Night and raised money for the project.

  2. On March 27, 2020, as a WWF volunteer, he called on everyone to protect nature, pay attention to snow leopard protection and biodiversity.

  3. On June 26, 2020, Allen served as the ambassador of the Qingdao Event to protect the quiet ocean and clean beach.
